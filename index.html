Kalooh User Manual

User Manual Developer Guide

Kallooh is a social media app that lets users post “notes” based on their current location and allows others in the area to view and comment on these notes.


While many social media apps exist to connect users, there are none that sufficiently emphasize location as a medium of communicating with people in a given area. Our app, Kalooh, aims to fill this niche by giving users a way to interact with their surroundings and see others reactions as well. By focusing on location-based interaction, we can connect users in a way that is more personal and visible to each user.

Log In

The Log In Screen is the first screen you will be greated with on Kalooh. Logging into Kalooh is optional. Users who do not log in are only allowed to view notes.


Users can create a Kalooh ccount through the app itself. You can log in with Twitter, Google+, or Facebook.

Home

The Home screen will display the map and all the notes in your area.


1. Home: Pressing the Home Icon will return you to the main map screen showed above.

2. Note List View: Pressing the Search Icon will bring up a menu that will display the notes in your area in a list view. You will then be able to search notes by keywords.

3. Create New Note: Pressing the Create Note Icon will display the prompt in order to create a note to post on the map.

4. Friends: Pressing the Friends Icon will bring up the menu that will allow you to manage your friends.

5. Notifications: Pressing the Notifications Icon will bring up all unread notifications.

6. Settings: Pressing the Settings Icon will open the settings menu.


and 7. Note: Pressing a Note Icon on the map screen will open up the note preview screen.

8. The note preview screen displays only the title of the note. Pressing the note again will bring you to the View Note screen.

View Note

Notes in Kalooh have several sections:

1. The first section contains the User Generated Title of the note.

2. Following the title is a short description/message that better describes the note.

3. The 3rd section contains the Like/Dislike button, the amount of likes/dislikes, the comment button, the comment button, and then the amount of comments on the note.

4. The last section is the comments section, containing all the comments on the page. At the bottom of the comments section is a prompt where you are allowed to type and add your own comment to the note.


1. Like: Pressing the “Like” Icon will add one like to this note, increasing its popularity to other users.

2. Dislike: Pressing the “Dislike” Icon will decrease the number of likes by 1, decreasing its popularity to other users.

3. Comment: Pressing the “Comment” icon or the “Add Comment...” prompt at the bottom of the page allows you to add a personal message onto the note. This comment will be displayed for other users to see.

4. Exit Button: Pressing the exit button closes the note and returns you to the previous screen.

List View

The notes displayed on the Home Screen can be displayed in a list-view. Each entry in this menu represents a note with a title and description. The notes are ordered by proximity, and only notes in your area will be displayed.


From here, you can search for notes using keywords on the top of this menu. Selecting a note will bring you to the same note view menu as selecting it from the Home Screen.

Create Note

The Note Creation Page is the form that will contain all the User Generated information of your note. Going through the Note Creation Page will result in a personalized note being placed on the map.


1. Enter Title...: Enter the title for your note here.

2. Whats on Your Mind?: Pressing anywhere in the prompt containing the Pencil Icon will allow you to type the message/description of your note.

3. Choose Location: Brings up screen which you can then use to choose the location of your note.

4. Private Switch: Toggling this switch will determine if your note will be only viewable to your friends, or to the public.

5. Post Button: Pressing this button will finalize your post and place the note on the map for your friends/public to see.


This is the "Choose Location" page. Pressing a spot on the map will drop the location/pin of your note there. The defualt location is your current location.


6. Longitude: Entering numbers into this prompt will allow you to fine tune the longitude better. It will also dynamically change with the location you select on the map screen.

7. Latitude: Entering numbers into this prompt will allow you to fine tune the latitude better. It will also dynamically change with the location you select on the map screen.

8. Select: Pressing this button will finalize the location and take you back to the note creation screen.

Friends:

The Friends menu will allow you to manage all of your friends. Friends are the people who will be able to see your private post. Each block entry in the friend menu represents 1 friend with their username stated to the left.


1. Search Prompt Entering a username in the prompt will perform a search on all users of Kalooh, allowing you to add users who are not currently your friend or to manage a user who is currently your friend.

2. Delete Friend Pressing the red X icon to the right of the friend entry will delete this user of your friends list. They will no longer be able to see your private notes.

View Notifications

The Notifications Page will display all unread notifications that are associated with your post. This includes comments, likes, and dislikes. Pressing a notification icon will take you to the post associated with that notification, and scroll you to the specific comment if possible.

Settings

The Settings Menu will allow you to access important information about Kalooh, manage your account details, and tweak your Kalooh experience to best fit your preferences.


  • 1. About Us: Pressing this link will take you to a page where all information about user privacy and Kalooh will be contained.
  • 2. Change Email: Pressing this link will take you to a page where you can change your email associated with your account log in.
  • 3. Change Password: Pressing this link will take you to a page where you can change your email associated with your account log in.
  • 4. Notifications: Toggling this switch will turn all notifications off or on.
  • 5. Friends Only: Toggling this switch will determine if notifications will only appear from interactions associated with friends.
  • 6. Only Friends Can See Post: Toggling this switch will determine if your post will always be set to private. When private, only your friends will be able to see the notes you post.
